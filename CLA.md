## How to sign

This Agreement is signed automatically when you open your first pull request to the Project. Our CLA bot — the [contributor-assistant/github-action](https://github.com/contributor-assistant/github-action) workflow running in this repository — will:

1. Comment on your pull request asking you to sign.
2. Ask you to reply with the comment: `I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA`.
3. Record your signature (GitHub identity, timestamp, commit SHAs) in the [Cogniplane/cla-signatures](https://github.com/Cogniplane/cla-signatures) registry repository, and unblock your pull request.

You only sign once. After that, all future pull requests from your GitHub account are pre-cleared.

If you are contributing on behalf of an employer or organization, please contact `licensing@cogniplane.ai` first — we will arrange a Corporate CLA instead.
